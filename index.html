About Me

はじめまして! My name is Yu-Han Yeh, and my English name is Judy. I am a student in the Information Systems Science and Engineering program at Ritsumeikan University.

In my free time, I enjoy studying with my friends. I also hope to create study groups where students with different interests, knowledge, and skills can help each other after class.

Creating a supportive study group was one of the goals I mentioned during an interview before entering Ritsumeikan University. I hope this website can support this goal by helping students understand and record their study habits.

After testing this website, I found that I usually study for about seven hours per day. Much of my study time is spent on Mathematics, Introduction to Information Systems Science, and Programming. These are important subjects in my university program.

I hope this website can help students like me who need a practical way to record their study time, expenses, progress, and weekly goals.

About This Website

This website helps university students track their weekly study time and lunch expenses.

Students can record:

  • Study Subject
  • Study Hours
  • Lunch Expense
  • Study Location

Go to the Portfolio page to enter your weekly records.

Go to the Trivia page to calculate the total study hours and lunch expenses.
